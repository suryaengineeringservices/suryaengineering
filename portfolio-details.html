FEA of Complete Pressure Vessel

Introduction

Pressure vessels are subjected to various loading conditions depending upon industrial applications. SURYA Engineering performed finite element analysis to validate the structural integrity and safety of the pressure vessel under operating loads.

The objective was to identify stress concentration regions, validate design safety, and ensure compliance with ASME standards using ANSYS Mechanical simulations.

Workflow

  • Design parameter and material property extraction.
  • Preparation of high-quality hex mesh.
  • Static structural analysis using ANSYS Mechanical.
  • Stress linearization and fatigue evaluation.
  • Engineering report preparation.

Challenges

  • Capturing weld regions accurately.
  • Managing mesh quality for complex geometry.
  • Stress linearization at critical junctions.
