FEA of Complete Pressure Vessel

Advanced structural and stress validation using ANSYS Mechanical

Introduction

Pressure vessels are subjected to various operating and loading conditions depending on industrial process requirements. SURYA Engineering performed finite element analysis to validate the structural integrity and stress distribution of the pressure vessel assembly.

The project involved evaluation of stress concentration regions, nozzle-shell junctions and weld areas under internal pressure loading conditions using ANSYS Mechanical simulation tools.

Workflow

  • Extraction of design parameters and material properties.
  • Preparation of structured hex-dominant mesh.
  • Static structural analysis using ANSYS Mechanical.
  • Stress linearization and fatigue assessment.
  • Result validation according to ASME standards.
  • Engineering report preparation with recommendations.

Challenges

  • Capturing accurate stress concentration at nozzle junctions.
  • Maintaining mesh quality for complex geometrical regions.
  • Stress linearization at weld locations.
  • Managing large model computational requirements.

Results

The simulation successfully validated the structural safety of the pressure vessel. Critical stress regions were identified and optimized. The final design met ASME stress limits and operational safety requirements.
